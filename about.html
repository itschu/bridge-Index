About Zoe Index Fund

As much as cryptos are currencies that could be used for our daily transactions they are also assets so one can acquire them and hold them in hopes they would appreciate with time. Not so many people are trained and educated to properly assess the crypto market and invest in the assets that have a good tendency to increase with time.

To solve this problem we are creating Zoe Index, the first index fund for crypto, An index fund is a portfolio of various crypto assets. instead of an individual purchasing one crypto and his entire portfolio is dependent on the success of that one portfolio, purchasing this token automatically invokes a smart contract that purchases all the crypto assets related to its index, by so doing you get the opportunity to diversify your portfolio. With this, your portfolio isn't dependent on one crypto asset but various crypto assets.

Frequently asked questions about Zoe Index

  • How Secure Are My Funds

    Building on blockchain technology security should be the least of your worries because the technology has been vetted by professionals to be almost invulnerable and currently the best in the worl.

  • How Does Zoe Index Work?

    When you purchase our tokens you firstly get the number of tokens you paid for then a smart contract is being invoked using the funds you deposited that automatically purchases all the crypto assets associated with our index.

  • Do I Have All The Assets The Smart Contract Acquires?

    Yes, you have the value for all of the crypto assets acquired but not the crypto-assets themselves so you can't spend just one of them, rather you are given a Zoe Index Token representing the value of your portfolio.

  • What Is An Index Fund?

    An index fund is a portfolio of stocks or bonds designed to mimic the composition and performance of a financial market index, Index funds seek to match the risk and return of the market, on the theory that in the long-term, the market will outperform any single investment.
